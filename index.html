Who We Are


International Agencies (Bd.) Ltd. (IABL) group is the largest healthcare provider of Bangladesh. IABL group(Established in 1977) has a long & Strong heritage of 46 years in healthcare sector of Bangladesh. IABL group has a very glorious history in Diabetes Field for more than 4 decades. IABL group is completely dedicated to healthcare industry. More than 46 years later, we remain committed to compliance & ethics through every aspect of our business and the people we serve starting with those who take our medicines and extending to health care professionals, employees and the communities in which we live. IABL group is marketing so many life saving drugs in Bangladesh to serve the community. Our prime objective is to serve the nation. We harness healthcare trends continuously offering innovative, convenient and safe products for all customers. IABL group represented so many multinationals in Bangladesh like Sclavo Diagnostics, Novo Nordisk, Swiss Serum & Vaccine Institute, Smith & Nephew (UK).

Presently IABL Group is the marketing & distribution partner of CSL Behring, Australia, Eli Lilly & Company, USA, Becton Dickinson (Diabetes Portfolio), USA, Danone Foods, France & Mega We Care, Thailand in Bangladesh. IABL group has expanded its business in 2016 by establishing its own pharmaceuticals industry which is a cGMP standard Pharma industry as per the guidelines of WHO & USFDA. IABL group has a very largest set up in Bangladesh covering 64 districts of Bangladesh through its strong distribution Network. More than 800 employees are currently working in IABL group. IABL group is committed to the highest standards of corporate conduct and responsibility. Our slogan is “First in Class & Best in Class”
